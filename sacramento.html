Sacramento

Sacramento is the capital city of the U.S. state of California and the seat of government of Sacramento County. Sacramento is the fastest-growing major city in California, owing to its status as a notable political center on the West Coast and as a major educational hub, home of California State University, Sacramento and UC Davis.
