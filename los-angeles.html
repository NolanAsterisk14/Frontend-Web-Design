Los Angeles

Los Angeles, often referred to by its initials L.A., is the most populous city in the U.S. state of California. Los Angeles has a diverse economy with a broad range of industries. Los Angeles is best known as the home of the Hollywood film industry, the world's largest by revenue; the city was an important site in the history of film. It also has one of the busiest container ports in the Americas.
